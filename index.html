Archetype Description

The Creator exists to give form to what does not yet exist. You don't merely solve problems — you reimagine them entirely. Your intelligence is both deeply aesthetic and deeply systemic, producing a rare combination of vision and execution that few people share. You function best when given creative autonomy paired with a clear sense of purpose. Without it, you either stall or overextend into too many directions at once.
